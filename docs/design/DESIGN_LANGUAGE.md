# Design Language

## Design Goal

ParMind should feel calm, intentional, and trustworthy.

The interface should disappear into the background, allowing golfers to focus on playing better golf rather than operating an app.

---

## Emotional Qualities

Every screen should feel:

- Calm
- Confident
- Focused
- Honest
- Premium

Never:

- Busy
- Flashy
- Gamified
- Noisy
- Stressful

---

## Visual Philosophy

Less, but better.

Whitespace is a feature.

Typography creates hierarchy.

Motion communicates intent.

Every visual element must earn its place.

---

## Interaction Philosophy

The fastest interaction is the one that doesn't require thought.

Large touch targets.

Minimal typing.

One-handed operation.

Clear next actions.

---

## Success Metric

If a golfer can use ParMind during a round without thinking about the interface, the design has succeeded.

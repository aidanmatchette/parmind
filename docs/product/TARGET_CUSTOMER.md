# Target Customer

## Primary Customer

ParMind is built for the serious amateur golfer.

Someone who:

- Plays at least twice per month.
- Actively wants to improve.
- Believes golf is as much mental as it is physical.
- Is willing to reflect on decisions instead of blaming bad shots.
- Values process over quick fixes.

Typical handicap:

5–20

## Not Our Customer

ParMind is not designed primarily for:

- Complete beginners learning the rules.
- Golfers who only want GPS yardages.
- Golfers looking solely for score tracking.
- Golfers who have no interest in improving their decision-making.

## Customer Promise

ParMind helps serious golfers become better decision-makers, leading to more consistent golf and lower scores over time.

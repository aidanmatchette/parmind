# Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

## Goal

Deliver the smallest possible product that genuinely helps golfers become better decision-makers during and after a round.

## Included

- Start a round
- Hole-by-hole scoring
- Decision tracking
- Mental game tracking
- Round summary
- Decision score
- Round history
- Offline support
- Installable PWA

## Explicitly Excluded

These ideas are intentionally postponed beyond the MVP:

- AI coaching
- Voice input
- Cloud sync
- User accounts
- Apple Watch
- Social features
- GPS distances
- Smartwatch support
- Advanced analytics
- Strokes Gained
- Handicap calculations
- Course database
- Tournament management

## MVP Success

A golfer finishes a round and says:

"I understand why I scored the way I did, and I know what to improve next round."

# Product Thesis

## Better Golf Through Better Decisions

Golfers spend countless hours trying to improve their swing.

Far fewer spend time improving the decisions they make before, during, and after every shot.

Yet the difference between a good round and a great round is often not a better swing—it is a better decision.

ParMind exists to help golfers become better decision-makers.

Rather than rewarding outcomes alone, ParMind reinforces disciplined thinking, intentional course management, emotional control, and commitment to a plan.

We believe that better decisions compound over time.

Better decisions become better habits.

Better habits become better golf.

ParMind is not simply a scorecard.

It is a decision improvement platform designed to help golfers think more clearly, play more intentionally, and continuously improve.

Every feature we build should support that mission.
